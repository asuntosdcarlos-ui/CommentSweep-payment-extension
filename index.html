How we use your data

CommentSweep uses Google's YouTube Data API to perform actions on behalf of the authenticated YouTube channel owner. Here is exactly what we access and why:

  • YouTube channel info — to identify your channel and use it as a unique account identifier.
  • Your videos list — to scan comments across all or selected videos.
  • Comments on your videos — to find and display comments matching your keywords.
  • Delete comments — only the comments you explicitly select and confirm for deletion.

We do not store your comments, videos, or personal data on any external server. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your YouTube channel ID is only used to verify your subscription status in our database.
